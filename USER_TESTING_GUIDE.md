# Doxa App — User Testing Guide

Thank you for helping us test the app! This guide walks you through the newest
features. You don't need any technical knowledge — just follow the steps and
tell us if anything looks wrong, confusing, or different from what's described.

## How to use this guide

- Work through each test in order. Each one has **steps to follow** and **what
  you should see**.
- If what you see matches the description, tick the box ✅.
- If something is different, broken, or confusing, make a note — even small
  things like a typo or a button in the wrong place are helpful.
- Use a **real email address you can check**, because one of the tests involves
  receiving an email.

**When you find a problem, please note:**
- What you were doing (which test / step)
- What you expected to happen
- What actually happened
- A screenshot if you can

> 💡 The app also has a built-in **Feedback** button (Test 4) — that's a great
> way to send us notes, and it automatically includes your phone details.

## Test 1 — Signing up for news & updates

There are **two** ways to sign up. Please test whichever applies to you.

### 1a. During first-time setup (new users)

If you are installing the app for the first time, you'll be guided through a few
welcome screens. One of them invites you to sign up for updates.

**Steps:**
1. Follow the welcome screens until you reach the "sign up for updates" screen.
2. Enter your **name**.
3. Enter your **email address**.
4. Notice the two tick-boxes — "Updates about my people group" and "Updates from
   Doxa". Both start ticked. Leave them, or untick if you prefer.
5. Tap **Sign up**.

**What you should see:**
- [ ] If you leave the name or email blank (or type an invalid email), the app
      warns you and won't continue.
- [ ] After a successful sign-up, a **thank-you message** appears telling you to
      **check your email to confirm your subscription**, showing the email you
      entered.
- [ ] You may also be offered the chance to **turn on notifications**.

### 1b. From Settings (any time)

**Steps:**
1. Go to **Settings** (gear/menu icon).
2. Tap **Sign up for updates**.
3. Fill in your **name** and **email**, choose the tick-boxes, and tap **Save**.

**What you should see:**
- [ ] The same **thank-you / check-your-email** message appears.
- [ ] Blank or invalid entries are rejected with a warning, just like above.

## Test 2 — Verifying your email

After signing up, we send a confirmation email. This test checks that the
verification works and that the app reflects your status correctly.

**Steps:**
1. Sign up (Test 1) using a real email address you can open.
2. Go to **Settings** and scroll down to the **Account** section.
3. Look at the email listed there.

**What you should see:**
- [ ] Your email appears **partly hidden** for privacy (for example
      `n***@g***.org`) — this is expected, we don't show the full address.
- [ ] It shows an **"Unverified"** label (amber ⚠️ icon) until you confirm it.

**Now confirm it:**
4. Open your email inbox and find the message from Doxa.
5. Tap the **confirmation / verify link** inside that email.
6. Return to the app, go to **Settings → Account** again (if it doesn't update,
   leave the Settings screen and come back).

**What you should see:**
- [ ] Your email now shows **"Verified"** with a green ✔️ icon.

### Resend button

If your email is still **Unverified**, there's a **Resend verification** option.

**Steps:**
7. With an unverified email showing, tap **Resend verification**.

**What you should see:**
- [ ] A brief message confirms the email was sent.
- [ ] The button then shows a **countdown** (about 60 seconds) and can't be
      tapped again until the countdown finishes. This is normal — it stops
      accidental duplicate emails.
- [ ] A new confirmation email arrives in your inbox.

## Test 3 — Your profile info

**Steps:**
1. Go to **Settings** and find the **Account** section (same place as Test 2).
2. Tap **View profile**.

**What you should see:**
- [ ] Your **web profile page opens in your phone's browser** (outside the app).
- [ ] The page loads correctly and shows information relating to you.
- [ ] You can return to the app normally afterwards.

---

## Test 4 — The feedback form

**Steps:**
1. Go to the **Home** screen.
2. Find the **Get Involved** card.
3. Tap the **Feedback** button (speech-bubble icon).

**What you should see:**
- [ ] A **feedback form opens in your phone's browser**.
- [ ] The form is **in the same language** your app is set to.
- [ ] You can type a message and submit it.

> The form quietly attaches some harmless details about your phone into the URL (model,
> operating system, app version) so we can understand any issues better. You
> don't need to type these in — please just leave them as they are.

**Optional — try in another language:**
4. In **Settings**, change the app **language**.
5. Open the feedback form again and check it appears in the new language.
